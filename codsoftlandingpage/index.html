About us

We're your go-to food delivery and restaurant discovery platform, dedicated to bringing delicious meals right to your doorstep. With a passion for convenience, quality, and variety, we're here to make your dining experience effortless and enjoyable. Join us in savoring the flavors of the world, one delivery at a time

WSwiggy is a leading online food delivery platform that has revolutionized the way people in India and select international cities access their favorite food. Founded in 2014 by Sriharsha Majety, Nandan Reddy, and Rahul Jaimini, Swiggy has rapidly grown to become one of the most trusted and convenient food delivery services in the country. The Swiggy platform offers an extensive network of restaurants, ranging from local eateries to popular chains, providing customers with a vast array of culinary choices. Users can easily browse menus, place orders, and track deliveries in real-time through the user-friendly mobile app or website. Swiggy's commitment to customer satisfaction is evident in its focus on prompt and reliable deliveries. The company employs a vast fleet of delivery partners, often referred to as "Swiggy delivery executives," who ensure that orders reach customers' doorsteps fresh and on time. In addition to its core food delivery service, Swiggy has expanded its offerings to include Swiggy Genie, which facilitates pick-up and drop-off services for a wide range of items, and Swiggy Pop, a curated menu of single-serve meals. With its innovative approach to food delivery, a commitment to quality, and a dedication to customer convenience, Swiggy has earned its place as a dominant player in India's food delivery industry and continues to shape the way people experience dining in the digital age.

Mutton briyani

Mutton Biryani, a timeless culinary masterpiece, is a fragrant and flavorful Indian dish that combines tender pieces of succulent mutton with aromatic spices and long-grain Basmati rice. Slow-cooked to perfection, it marries the richness of marinated meat with the fragrant blend of cardamom, cloves, and saffron. The result is a symphony of textures and tastes that tantalize the palate. Mutton Biryani isn't just a meal; it's an enchanting gastronomic journey, where each bite tells a story of tradition, heritage, and the love of food. Savor this royal dish and immerse yourself in its culinary charm.

beef briyani

Beef Biryani, a tantalizing fusion of tender beef chunks and aromatic spices, is a culinary masterpiece that ignites the senses. Slow-cooked to perfection, the beef absorbs the rich flavors of saffron, cumin, and cardamom, creating a symphony of taste. Each bite is a journey through the fragrant basmati rice, interspersed with succulent meat and garnished with crispy fried onions and fresh coriander. This dish is a celebration of tradition and taste, inviting you to savor the perfect balance of textures and spices that make Beef Biryani an unforgettable culinary delight.

Chicken rice

Chicken rice is a mouthwatering dish that tantalizes taste buds worldwide. This culinary delight combines tender, succulent chicken pieces, perfectly seasoned and cooked to perfection, with fragrant, fluffy rice infused with aromatic herbs and spices. It's a harmonious marriage of flavors and textures, often accompanied by a delectable dipping sauce that adds an extra layer of savoriness. Whether you savor it in Singapore's Hainanese style or enjoy variations from around the globe, chicken rice is a universally adored dish that showcases the culinary artistry of balancing simplicity and deliciousness.
